## prompts

Contains reusable AI prompts organized by task type.

### meetings
Prompts related to meeting processing such as summaries, action items, or decision extraction.

### analysis
Prompts used to analyze product information, feedback, or requirements.

### documentation
Prompts used to generate structured product documentation.

### prioritization
Prompts used for product prioritization frameworks and roadmap evaluation.

## templates

Reusable templates used by prompts.

Examples:
- meeting minutes templates
- prioritization spreadsheets
- PRD templates

## workflows

Documents multi-step workflows combining multiple prompts.

## examples

Example inputs and outputs demonstrating how prompts are used.

## docs

Internal documentation for repository management.
